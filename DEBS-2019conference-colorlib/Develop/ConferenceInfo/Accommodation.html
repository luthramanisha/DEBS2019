Accommodation

Conference Hotels

The 13th ACM International Conference on Distributed and Event-Based Systems has arranged special prices for accommodation with the Welcome Hotel directly located besides the conference venue. Note that the availability is limited in number and requires a booking ahead of May 26th, 2019.

Welcome Hotel Darmstadt

Official website: Welcome Hotel Darmstadt

Karolinenplatz 4, 64289 Darmstadt

  • In walking distance (140m) to the venue.
  • Cost: Comfort Double Room Rate (1 person) incl. breakfast (122€ per night).
  • PLEASE QUOTE DEBS DA 2019 when making your booking.
  • For reservations contact the hotel directly via info.dar@welcome-hotels.com or phone +49-6151-39140.
  • The discount is transferable to other available categories.

    • Other local accommodation
