SAP Sponsored Travel Support

SAP supports DEBS with a travel fund that is meant to support the attendance at the conference. Applicants can obtain a grant of roughly 500â‚¬ covering partially cost for conference registration and travel.

The selection criteria depend on

  • the need of the person, e.g. the institutions has limited capacity of supporting their Ph.D. students and staff members
  • the excellence of the person
  • relevance of the academic track to the topics of the conference
  • contribution to the conference

    • For application, please send a short letter/email (no longer than 1 page) to the general chairs introducing:

  • the reason and need for applying for the travel funding
  • indicating the research interest in DEBS 2019
  • indicating whether the person has an accepted contribution at DEBS 2019 Please, also attach a CV

    • As the travel funds is limited, applications are ideally submitted ahead of May 15th.

    DEBS will be held back-to-back with SIGMOD 2019 in Europe.
